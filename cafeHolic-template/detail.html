Arrived last night... I could not WAIT to cup this coffee up and give it a taste this morning! So i made an overnight cold brew for my partner! We had our wishes this morning... but were somewhat let down. Brightness and fruitiness are both quite underwhelming. The coffee is roasted "dry" ie., marginally dark but with no lacquering and unfortunately produces a final, cupped or cold-brewed, product that has a definite "green" aftertaste. NOT at all satisfying... it's ok... definitely not great.
